Kredila Education Loan Policy – India

Kredila offers education loans for students pursuing higher education in India and abroad. This document outlines the policy framework for domestic education loans.

1. Overview
Kredila's education loan scheme supports students admitted to recognized institutions in India. The policy covers tuition fees, hostel charges, books, equipment, and travel expenses for study tours.

2. Eligible Courses
• Professional Degrees: B.Tech, M.Tech, MBBS, BDS, MBA, MCA, BBA, etc.
• Graduation and Post-Graduation from UGC/AICTE recognized institutions
• Premier institutes: IITs, IIMs, NITs, AIIMS, NLUs, XLRI, ISB
• Diploma and certification courses from recognized institutes

3. Loan Amount & Interest Rates
• Tier-1 Institutes: Up to INR 50 Lakhs unsecured (no collateral)
• Tier-2 Institutes: Up to INR 7.5 Lakhs unsecured (as per IBA Model Scheme)
• Above limits: Collateral required (property, FD, LIC)
• Interest Rate: Repo Linked Lending Rate (RLLR) + spread (currently 9.5% - 12%)
• Moratorium: Course duration + 6 months or 12 months post-employment
• Repayment: Up to 15 years

4. Disbursement Policy
• Direct disbursement to institution for tuition and hostel fees
• Funds for books and equipment credited to student account
• Disbursement in installments per semester/trimester
• Minimum 48-72 hours for domestic disbursements
• Documentation: Signed agreement, e-NACH mandate, fee invoice required

5. Tax Benefits
• Interest deduction under Section 80E of Income Tax Act
• Available for up to 8 years from start of repayment

6. Collateral and Security
• Unsecured: Up to INR 50 Lakhs (Tier-1) / INR 7.5 Lakhs (Tier-2)
• Secured: Property, Fixed Deposit, or third-party guarantee
• Margin: 5-15% for loans above unsecured limit
