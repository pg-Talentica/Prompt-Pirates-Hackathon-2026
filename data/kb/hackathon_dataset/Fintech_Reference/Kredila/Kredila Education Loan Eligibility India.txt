Kredila Education Loan Eligibility Checklist – India

Eligibility requirements for Kredila education loans for domestic studies.

1. Applicant Requirements
• Indian citizen
• Age: 16-35 years at the time of loan sanction
• Admission to a recognized institution (UGC/AICTE/NAAC accredited)
• Co-applicant mandatory (parent or guardian with regular income)

2. Academic Eligibility
• Valid admission letter from the institute
• For continuing students: Previous semester mark sheets
• Minimum academic performance as per course requirements
• No default history in any previous loan

3. Financial Documentation
• Income proof of co-applicant (ITR, salary slips, Form 16)
• Bank statements (6-12 months)
• Property documents (if collateral required)
• Margin money: 5-15% for loans above unsecured limit

4. Identity & Address Proof
• Aadhaar card (mandatory)
• PAN card (mandatory, must be linked to Aadhaar)
• Passport size photographs
• Address proof (utility bill, rent agreement)

5. Institution Requirements
• Institute must be recognized by UGC/AICTE/NAAC
• Fee structure and break-up from institution
• Demand letter or fee invoice for disbursement
• Institute bank account details for direct transfer

6. Pre-Disbursement Checklist
• Signed loan agreement (e-sign via Aadhaar/DigiLocker)
• e-NACH or e-Mandate for EMI deduction
• Post-dated cheques (if applicable)
• Insurance (optional but recommended)
