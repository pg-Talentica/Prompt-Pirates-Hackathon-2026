Kredila Student Loan Scheme – India

Kredila offers comprehensive education loans under the Kredila Student Loan Scheme for studies in India and abroad.

1. Scheme Overview
• Scheme Name: Kredila Student Loan Scheme
• Purpose: Tuition fees, hostel, books, equipment, travel for education
• Domestic and international studies covered

2. Loan Amount & Interest
• Domestic: Up to INR 1.5 Crore for premier institutes
• Unsecured: Up to INR 7.5 Lakhs (IBA Model Scheme); Up to INR 40 Lakhs for IITs/IIMs
• Interest: EBR (External Benchmark Rate) + spread; currently 9.15% - 11.15%
• Moratorium: Course period + 12 months post-employment
• Repayment: Up to 15 years

3. Eligibility
• Indian citizen; age 18+ (minor with guardian as co-applicant)
• Admission to recognized institution (UGC/AICTE/NAAC)
• Co-applicant mandatory (parent/guardian with income)
• No default in previous loans

4. Disbursement
• Direct transfer to institution for tuition
• Installment-wise per semester/trimester
• SLA: 7-10 working days after document completion
• Documentation: Agreement, e-NACH, fee demand letter required

5. Collateral
• Up to INR 7.5 Lakhs: No collateral
• INR 7.5 Lakhs - 40 Lakhs: Third-party guarantee
• Above 40 Lakhs: Property mortgage or collateral

6. Tax Benefits
• Section 80E: Interest deduction for up to 8 years
