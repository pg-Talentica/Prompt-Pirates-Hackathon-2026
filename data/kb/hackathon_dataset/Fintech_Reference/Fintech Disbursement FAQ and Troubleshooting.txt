Kredila Education Loan Disbursement – FAQ and Troubleshooting

Common queries for Kredila education loan disbursement and troubleshooting.

1. Why is my loan not disbursed for 15+ days?

Possible reasons (check in order):

A. Repayment Mandate (e-NACH/e-Mandate) Not Active
   • All banks require active NACH/mandate for EMI deduction before disbursement
   • Solution: Complete mandate registration; verify status in net banking
   • Typical resolution: 2-3 business days after mandate success

B. Document Incomplete
   • Fee demand letter from institution not received
   • Signed agreement or e-sign pending
   • Name mismatch between PAN/Aadhaar and admission letter
   • Solution: Submit missing docs; Affidavit of Identity for name mismatch
   • Resolution: 3-5 days after document receipt

C. PAN Not Linked to Aadhaar
   • RBI mandate for all financial transactions
   • Solution: Link at incometax.gov.in
   • Resolution: Same day

D. Margin Money or Top-Up Pending
   • For loans above unsecured limit, margin (5-15%) must be paid first
   • Solution: Pay as per sanction letter
   • Resolution: 1-2 days after payment

E. CIBIL or Credit Issue
   • New default in co-applicant or student
   • Credit refresh required before each disbursement
   • Solution: Resolve dues; wait 7-14 days for CIBIL update

F. Institute Confirmation Delayed
   • Bank verifies fee with institute
   • Solution: Institute must send demand letter or confirmation
   • Resolution: 5-7 days depending on institute

G. Post-Dated Cheques (PDC)
   • Required for unsecured loans
   • Solution: Submit PDCs at branch
   • Resolution: 3-5 days after validation

H. LRS Limit (International Loans)
   • Annual limit $250,000 USD
   • Solution: Check utilization; may defer to next FY

2. What documents are needed for disbursement?

• Signed loan agreement (e-sign via Aadhaar/DigiLocker)
• Active e-NACH or e-Mandate for EMI
• Fee demand letter or invoice from institution
• Institute bank account details (for direct transfer)
• Post-dated cheques (if applicable)
• CIBIL refresh within 24-48 hours of disbursement

3. How long does disbursement take?

• Kredila: Domestic 24-48 hours; International 48-72 hours
• Standard SLA: 7-10 working days after complete documentation
• Premier institutes: May have expedited processing

4. Whom to contact for delayed disbursement?

• Start: Branch/Relationship Manager with loan reference number
• Escalate: Customer care if no response in 7 days
• Central grievance: Bank's grievance email for delay > 15 days
• RBI Ombudsman: If bank does not resolve in 30 days
