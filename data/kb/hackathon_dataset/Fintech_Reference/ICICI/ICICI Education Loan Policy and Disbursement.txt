ICICI Bank Education Loan – Policy and Disbursement

ICICI Bank offers education loans for studies in India and abroad under competitive terms.

1. Loan Overview
• Domestic: Up to INR 1 Crore for premier institutes
• Unsecured: Up to INR 50 Lakhs (Tier-1); Up to INR 7.5 Lakhs (Tier-2) as per IBA
• Interest: Repo Linked; 9.5% - 11.5% p.a.
• Moratorium: Course duration + 6 months
• Repayment: Up to 15 years

2. Eligibility
• Indian citizen; age 16-35
• Admission to UGC/AICTE/NAAC recognized institution
• Co-applicant (parent/guardian) with income proof
• Clean credit history

3. Disbursement Policy
• Direct transfer to institution for fees
• Installment-wise disbursement per semester
• SLA: 48-72 hours for domestic after document completion
• Requirements: Signed agreement, e-NACH, fee demand letter, CIBIL refresh

4. Reasons for Disbursement Delay – ICICI Specific

• e-NACH/E-mandate not active: Re-initiate mandate; disbursement blocked until active
• Document incomplete: Demand letter, fee invoice, or institute confirmation pending
• PAN-Aadhaar not linked: Mandatory; link immediately for same-day fix
• Margin money pending: Pay 5-15% margin for loans above unsecured limit
• CIBIL refresh pending: Credit check done 24-48 hours before disbursement
• Institute verification: ICICI may verify fee directly; institute delay adds 3-5 days
• Name mismatch: Affidavit of Identity if PAN/Aadhaar name differs from admission letter

• iMobile app: Track loan status
• Escalation: grievance@icicibank.com for delays > 10 days
